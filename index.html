About ICCII-2020

Welcome to the official website of the International Conference on Computational Intelligence and Informatics-2020(ICCII-2020)will be held during 14th & 15th February 2020 at JNTU, Hyderabad. ICCII 2020, is to bring together innovative academics and industrial experts in the field of Computer Science and Electronics Engineering to a common forum. Accepted Papers will be published in Scoupus indexed International Journal of Innovative Technology and Exploring Engineering (IJITEE).

About JNTU

Situated in the heart of the city at Kukatpally, Hyderabad a major landmark- Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad has an environment of academic excellence with A Grade accreditation by NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council). Bringing the spark of knowledge to young minds and instilling a new confidence and vigour to face the world, it is providing quality education for nearly 4 decades. The University is dedicated to provide for the advancement of learning and knowledge in Engineering & Technology, Physical and Social Sciences,both by teaching, and research.

About JNTUHCEH

The College was established as Nagarjuna Sagar Engineering College in 1965. With the formation of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University on 2nd October 1972, it became a constituent college of the JNT University and was later renamed as JNTUH College of Engineering, Hyderabad. The college is offering technical education in major engineering fields and is sustaining, improving its repute of undergraduate programmes, expanding need based post graduate and research programmmes with global outlook, synergizing teaching and research for social relevance.
